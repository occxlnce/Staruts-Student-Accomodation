About Staruts Students Accommodation

Staruts Student Accommodation offers a purpose built student accommodation in Pretoria, Johannesburg, Polokwane and Cape Town. Your studies, your social life, your whole experience of university. It starts with your home, it is more than just student accommodation with a difference; it’s a unique experience, it’s a lifestyle and it’s affordable. Our student accommodation development philosophy will embark on providing accommodation that offers strategic choice of locations, coupled with cutting-edge design and exceptional quality buildings. We know it’s really hard to find great quality accommodation close to universities that is affordable.We also know that a safe, well looked after place to live is even more difficult to find. That’s where Staruts Student Accommodation comes in.

Our Student Accommodation is based on a

7-PRINCIPLE PHILOSOPHY


Quality Accommodation

We provide a comfortable environment where students can study and play effectively, adhering to higher education standards and universities' requirements.

Convenient Locations

All accommodations will include a bus service with an organized schedule, ensuring convenience and responsibility towards our customers and communities.

Safety & Security

Our facilities feature advanced security measures, including CCTV, 24-hour security, and biometric access for all students, ensuring a safe and secure environment.

Health & Safety

We comply with national and municipal health and safety regulations and maintain Certificates of Compliance to ensure a safe living environment for students.

Affordability

We offer great value, providing comfortable, quality accommodation at affordable prices so that every student can enjoy their time with us.

Great Student Experiences

Our goal is to provide market-leading facilities that create a home-away-from-home experience, allowing students to focus on their education while enjoying their journey.

ResLife Student Support Program

ResLife promotes peer interaction and involvement in extracurricular activities, enhancing the overall student experience and leading to greater success.
