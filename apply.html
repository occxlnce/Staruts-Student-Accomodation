  • Please provide CLEAR copies of the following, as applicable (skip those documents which are not):

    - Letter of Confirmation of Bursary.
    - NSFAS Confirmation Letter or Financial Aid View from your UP Portal showing NSFAS Funding.

    - You are Required to make a full payment of a period of 3 months in advance.
    - Proof of Payment must be attached along with your Lease.
    - The admin will contact you for approval and allocation a Room and set a date to move in.

    3.For Parents / Guardians / Sponsor
    - Identification Document (RSA).
    - Proof of Residence.

  • FULL BURSARY HOLDERS
    If you are a bursary student, we require a letter from the bursar confirming the full Rand value of the bursary awarded to you, the date of disbursement and if payment will be made directly to Staruts Students Accommodation or via your university student account. Kindly provide us with a letter to this effect. If you are receiving a bursary of less than R50 000 for tuition and accommodation, the shortfall must be paid upfront.

    NFSAS bursary holders should note that NSFAS only covers the residence fees up to a capped limit per year, students are liable for the shortfall before taking up accommodation.

    PART BURSARY HOLDERS
    NFSAS bursary holders should note that NSFAS only covers the residence fees up to a certain capped limit per year. NSFAS students will only be accepted after the signing of their sponsorship agreement and the residence approval amount is confirmed.
